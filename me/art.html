Background

I haven't drawn much lately but creating art is something that I wish I could do for the rest of my life. I have been creating art
since I was a kid and I have been drawing digitally since ~2018.
Here is my artstation if you want to check out some of my works: ArtStation

AI Art Concerns

Having some coding background, I am interested in new developments in AI that could help artists better express their ideas like
Stable Diffusion. I have trained a couple of loRAs myself and I am excited to see what the future holds for AI art. I do understand
the concerns of AI taking over the jobs of artists but I believe that AI is just a tool and not a replacement for the creative minds behind the art.

Drawn by me, CSP, no AI tools.
